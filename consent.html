How We Collect Consent

VelocityAI collects verbal consent during AI-assisted phone calls. Before any text message is sent, our AI agent verbally states the following script:

Verbal Consent Script:

"Before we wrap up, would you like to receive a text confirmation of your appointment or call summary? By saying yes, you agree to receive a text message from VelocityAI. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Would you like to receive this confirmation?"

A text message is only sent when the caller verbally confirms consent by saying yes or an equivalent affirmative response.

Types of Messages Sent

  • Appointment confirmation messages
  • Call summary messages
  • Follow-up information requested during the call

We do not send marketing or promotional messages.

Opt-Out Instructions

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
