SMS Opt-In & Consent

VelocityAI sends SMS messages only to individuals who have provided explicit consent. By providing your phone number to our AI agents, you agree to receive appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-up messages via SMS.

Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Message frequency varies.

Our SMS opt-in process works as follows:

1
Customer initiates contact — A customer calls our AI receptionist or interacts with our AI agent to request a service or appointment.
2
Agent requests phone number — Our AI agent asks: "What's the best phone number for a text confirmation?" — making clear that a text message will be sent.
3
Customer provides verbal consent — By providing their phone number, the customer explicitly consents to receive SMS messages related to their request.
4
Confirmation sent — An SMS confirmation is sent immediately, including opt-out instructions. Example: "Hi [Name]! Your appointment is confirmed for [date] at [time]. Reply STOP to opt out."

SMS messages sent by VelocityAI include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Follow-up summaries after AI agent conversations
  • Requests for additional information (e.g., email address for calendar invites)

All SMS messages include opt-out instructions. To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP. To get help, reply HELP or contact us at erobin456@gmail.com.
